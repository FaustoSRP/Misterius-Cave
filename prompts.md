# Prompts de Imágenes — La Cueva Misteriosa

Cada prompt está diseñado para generar una ilustración de escena del juego.
**Estilo general:** Arte digital oscuro y atmosférico, estilo fantasía subterránea, paleta dominante de negros, grises oscuros, y acentos en dorado/ámbar. Perspectiva en primera persona del explorador mirando hacia adelante dentro de la cueva. Iluminación dramática con antorchas o bioluminiscencia.

---

## 🚪 Entrada a la Cueva (Pantalla Inicial)

> A dark and imposing cave entrance carved into a massive rocky cliff face, surrounded by twisted ancient trees and hanging vines. The cave mouth is a tall arch shape, pitch black inside, with faint golden-amber light glowing from deep within. A worn stone path leads to the entrance. Misty atmosphere, twilight sky in the background. Dark fantasy digital art style, moody and mysterious. First-person perspective approaching the entrance.

---

## Profundidad Baja (0m+)

### 🪨 low_1 — Gran Derrumbe

> Inside a dark cave tunnel, a massive rockfall completely blocks the path ahead. Huge jagged boulders and crumbled stone piled high from floor to ceiling. Dust particles float in the air, caught in the faint amber torchlight. Small gaps between rocks hint at a passage beyond. Dark fantasy digital art, dramatic lighting, first-person perspective facing the collapse.

---

### 🕳️ low_2 — Fosa Profunda

> A deep, dark chasm splitting the cave floor in two, stretching from wall to wall. The pit is bottomless and pitch black, with jagged rock edges. A faint eerie blue glow emanates from the depths. The narrow cave passage continues on the far side. Stalactites hang from above. Dark fantasy digital art, vertigo-inducing perspective looking down into the abyss, amber torchlight on the near edge.

---

### 🦇 low_3 — Colonia de Murciélagos Gigantes

> A vast cave chamber with hundreds of giant bats hanging from the ceiling, their eyes glowing red in the darkness. Some are mid-flight with huge leathery wings spread wide, swooping toward the viewer. Guano-covered stalagmites below. The cave walls are rough and glistening wet. Dark fantasy digital art, chaotic and threatening atmosphere, amber torchlight illuminating the swarm, first-person perspective looking up.

---

### 🌿 low_4 — Raíces Espinosas

> A cave floor completely covered in thick, twisted thorny roots with sharp barbs, glowing faintly with a sickly green bioluminescence. The roots creep up the walls and hang from the ceiling. The path ahead is choked with these dangerous plants. Dark fantasy digital art, oppressive and claustrophobic atmosphere, amber torchlight casting long shadows through the entangled roots, first-person perspective.

---

## Profundidad Media (100m+)

### 🗿 mid_1 — Golem de Barro

> A towering mud golem standing guard on an ancient crumbling stone bridge over an underground river. The golem is massive, humanoid, made of wet dark clay and stone, with glowing amber runes etched into its body. Its fists are clenched. The bridge is narrow with no railings, ancient carved pillars on each side. Dark fantasy digital art, imposing and monumental, warm amber glow from the runes contrasting the cold dark cave, first-person perspective facing the golem.

---

### 📜 mid_2 — Inscripción Antigua

> A massive sealed stone door deep inside a cave, covered in intricate ancient glowing golden runes and mysterious symbols. The door has no handle, only the carved inscriptions. Faint magical energy pulses from the symbols. The surrounding cave walls are smooth and clearly worked by ancient hands. Dark fantasy digital art, mystical and arcane atmosphere, golden light emanating from the runes illuminating the chamber, first-person perspective facing the sealed door.

---

### ☠️ mid_3 — Gas Tóxico

> A cave passage filled with thick, swirling toxic green and yellow gas rising from cracks in the rocky floor. Visibility is low, the gas creates an ominous haze. Dead cave creatures (insects, small bats) lie on the ground. The walls are stained with mineral deposits. Dark fantasy digital art, suffocating and dangerous atmosphere, sickly green-yellow tones mixing with amber torchlight, first-person perspective peering through the poisonous haze.

---

### 👁️ mid_4 — Ecos de Locura

> A surreal, distorted cave tunnel where the walls appear to shift and melt. Ghostly translucent faces emerge from the rock walls, whispering with open mouths. Reality bends — the floor curves upward, stalactites grow sideways. The explorer's shadow on the wall is twisted and monstrous, different from the viewer's pose. Dark fantasy digital art with surrealist elements, psychological horror atmosphere, unstable purple and amber lighting, disorienting first-person perspective.

---

## Profundidad Alta (200m+)

### 👾 high_1 — Bestia de Sombras

> A massive creature made entirely of living darkness lurking in absolute blackness. Only its silhouette is barely visible — towering, multi-limbed, with dozens of glowing white eyes scattered across its form. Tendrils of shadow reach toward the viewer. The cave walls are consumed by darkness, the explorer's torchlight is being swallowed and dimmed. Dark fantasy digital art, pure terror and dread, extreme contrast between fading amber light and consuming darkness, first-person perspective.

---

### ✨ high_2 — Guardián Arcano

> An ethereal, floating humanoid guardian made of pure arcane energy, radiating blinding golden and blue magical light. It wears ancient ornate armor that seems forged from crystal and starlight. Arcane sigils orbit around it. The cave chamber is enormous, carved with ancient architecture — pillars, arches, and a grand floor mosaic. Dark fantasy digital art, epic and awe-inspiring, intense magical illumination flooding the chamber, first-person perspective looking up at the guardian.

---

### 🌀 high_3 — Camino al Vacío

> The cave path crumbling and falling away into an infinite dark void below. Chunks of rock break off and tumble into nothingness. The remaining path is a narrow, unstable ledge along the cave wall. Beyond the void, faint distant lights twinkle like underground stars. The sense of scale is overwhelming and vertigo-inducing. Dark fantasy digital art, existential dread, vast empty space, amber light from behind the viewer barely reaching the crumbling edge, first-person perspective at the precipice.

---

### 🪞 high_4 — Tu Yo Corrompido

> Inside a dark crystalline cave chamber, the explorer faces a dark mirror version of themselves. The corrupted copy is identical but wrong — skin cracked with glowing red veins, eyes burning crimson, a sinister smile, shadow tendrils emanating from their body. They stand in a mirrored pose. The cave walls are made of dark reflective crystal, showing infinite distorted reflections. Dark fantasy digital art, unsettling doppelgänger horror, split amber and red lighting — warm on the viewer's side, blood red on the copy's side, first-person perspective facing the corrupted self.

---

## 🏆 Victoria (Pantalla Final)

> An explorer emerging from a dark cave into brilliant golden sunlight. The cave exit opens to a breathtaking vista — a lush valley bathed in warm light, mountains in the distance, a sky full of golden clouds. The explorer stands at the threshold, silhouetted against the light, arms slightly raised in triumph. Ancient carved pillars frame the exit. Behind them, the darkness of the cave. Dark fantasy transitioning to epic high fantasy, triumphant and emotional, dramatic golden backlighting creating a halo effect, cinematic wide shot from slightly behind the explorer.
